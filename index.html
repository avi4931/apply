About ISBR Group

One of the motivating elements at ISBR Group, Bangalore, has been fostering bright young minds in the proper manner, pointing them in the direction of opportunities, and providing the much-needed guidance for establishing careers and beyond.

The group was founded under the Bangalore Educational Trust and has since built a number of reputable academic institutions that provide undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral degrees in a variety of subjects, including management, aviation, science, technology, law, and commerce.

ISBR, which is situated in Bangalore, the nation's IT center, offers its graduates a plethora of chances for immersive, real-world learning. Because of this, ISBR students are significantly more capable of becoming responsible, socially conscious, and skillful citizens. The strong industry engagement at ISBR is demonstrated by the AICTE-CII Survey's "Best Industry Linked Institute" award. ISBR is a strong proponent of the "learning-by-doing" methodology. Numerous venues have been developed to improve our graduates' experiential learning, including projects, consulting, live projects, internships, business visits, moot court sessions for law students, guest lectures and workshops by distinguished industry experts, and mentorship by corporate practitioners.
